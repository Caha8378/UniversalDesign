Thought of the day


Everyone wants to be a dog, but if you really think about it wouldn't you rather be a cat? I dont have a cat, I have a dog, however, I think this topic needs some deeper analysis. Most people don't even know what they want. Or if they do, how do we know that we want the right things. This brings me to my original point of why we admire dogs so much. They live the simple life, they appreciate the little things. That sounds great, but what if the highlight of your day is when your owner finally comes home. Dogs can be content. Humans however...
