Rules and Regulations

Welcome to the NBA Stock Market! Buy up to 5 stocks and the values of the stock change based on what other users are buying.

Stocks liquify after the end of the season. If your team stock gets a top 4 pick, you lose the stock and they reset at a new value. If your team stock wins the NBA championship, your stock liquifies for twice its value.
